Privacy Policy

Offroad Tourist Coach Driving

Effective: March 2026

Our Commitment to You

Offroad Tourist Coach Driving ("the App", "we") is built by Crazy Cross Games. We respect your privacy. This document describes how we handle information when you use our mountain bus driving simulation. By installing or using the App, you accept these practices.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or device-related data. Specifically, we do not gather:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, address)
  • Identifiers (device ID, advertising ID, IP address)
  • Location or geolocation information
  • Gameplay or usage statistics (scores, levels, session duration)
  • Any other information that could identify you or your device

Data Stored Only on Your Device

Settings, progress, and preferences (e.g. sound options, unlocked content) may be saved on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties. You can delete it by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Links and Services

We do not integrate analytics, ads, or social plugins that collect your data. If you follow a link from the App or this site (e.g. to the App Store), that destination has its own privacy policy, which we do not control.

Age Rating and Young Players

The App is rated 13+. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone. Because we do not collect personal data, our approach is consistent with applicable privacy laws, including those relating to children (e.g. COPPA, GDPR where relevant).

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Effective" date above will be updated when we do. We recommend checking this page from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
